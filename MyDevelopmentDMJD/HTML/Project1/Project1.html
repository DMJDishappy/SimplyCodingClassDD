About Me


Introduction


Hello visitors of my lovely site! My name is Dakota, and I’m from Las Vegas – yes, that Vegas. I am obligated to defend my home city and inform you that it’s a lot more than the strip, and has lots of fun stuff that don’t involve gambling (or the other things you think of when you think Vegas). I’m currently at the Job Corps in San Diego, which is much wetter than I prefer. It’s already rained more here than I’d see in a year back home, and I’ve been here for 2 months. Like what is wrong with you San Diego. The sky is not supposed to open up that often. Get it together. Also, I have two questions for foggy mornings: Why do you exist? and Who came up with this? I just want to talk.

  3. I learned piano, but I am very out of practice. It is also hard.
  4. I’m a published author! I am better at getting poems written then stories (it’s the length), and a poem I entered into a contest got published as an honorable mention.
