Flamingos on Fire

Pretty but Dangerous

Flaming flamingos cannot be differentiated from a normal flamingo until they set themselves on fire. The fire does no damage to the flamingo nor other flaming flamingos. Surprisingly, normal flamingos are also immune to the flames. Humans are not immune. Treat flamingos with an abundance of caution and keep your children from approaching them.

WARNING! USE CAUTION!
